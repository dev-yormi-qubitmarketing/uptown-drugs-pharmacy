    • Customer Service

    Q. Do I have to make an appointment to see a pharmacist?

    No. We are open to the public during business hours and welcome walk-ins.

    Q. Do you have medical counseling available?

    Our trained medical staff is here for all prescription and medical questions. We have specialists that can assist with non-narcotic pain management, medical therapy management and prescription counseling.

    Payment

    Q. Do you accept insurance?

    We want coverage for everyone in our community so we offer insurance coverage and accept all forms of payment.

    Q. Can I pay for my prescription with a check?

    Yes. We accept all forms of payment.

    Delivery

    Q. Do you deliver medications?

    Yes. We delivery to both home and work locations.

    Q. How long do medication deliveries take to arrive?

    You will receive your medication within 3-5 business days, provided all insurance and doctor’s information goes through.
